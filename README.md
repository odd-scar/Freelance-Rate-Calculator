# SoloRate launch notes

This project is a static site aimed at monetizing freelancer pricing searches.

## What is included
- `index.html`: homepage with rate calculator, quote builder, affiliate sections, and lead capture area
- `freelance-pricing-guide.html`: starter SEO article to begin building topical authority
- `styles.css`: shared visual system and responsive layout
- `script.js`: calculator logic, copy-to-clipboard support, and local lead capture demo
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`: basic SEO launch assets

## Fastest path to revenue
1. Replace `https://example.com` in `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` with your real domain.
2. Swap placeholder partner URLs for your affiliate links.
3. Connect the lead form to an email platform such as ConvertKit or Beehiiv.
4. Publish supporting articles for specific niches: designers, developers, consultants, copywriters, photographers.
5. Submit the sitemap in Google Search Console after deploy.
