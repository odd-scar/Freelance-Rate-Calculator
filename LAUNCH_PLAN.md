# SoloRate launch and monetization brief

## Positioning
SoloRate should launch as a quote-first tool for freelancers and side hustles:

`Create freelance quotes faster with smart pricing built in.`

That is stronger than positioning it as only a rate calculator because the visible outcome is more valuable and easier to share.

## Why this niche is attractive now
- The U.S. Census reported 30.4 million nonemployer businesses in 2023.
- Upwork reported skilled knowledge freelancers generated $1.5 trillion in earnings in 2024.
- Upwork also reported rising demand for human-centric freelance skills like video editing, QA, and project management in 2025.

## Competitor categories to target
- Quote / proposal workflow tools: HoneyBook, Bonsai
- Invoicing / accounting tools: FreshBooks
- General freelancer workflow tools and templates

## Fastest monetization path this week
1. Keep the homepage focused on quote generation and printable exports.
2. Replace placeholder partner links with tracked affiliate links.
3. Publish 3 supporting pages:
   - photography quote template
   - freelance quote generator for consultants
   - best proposal software for freelancers
4. Add an email capture automation that delivers a quote pack.
5. Put partner CTAs immediately after quote creation and PDF export.

## Programs worth testing first
- FreshBooks affiliate program
- Bonsai affiliate program
- HoneyBook referral / affiliate program

## First-week metrics
- quote builder visits
- quote exports
- partner link clicks
- email signups
- revenue per 100 visitors

## Near-term product improvements after launch
- niche-specific quote language packs
- logo lockups and theme styles
- multiple page quote PDFs
- saved quote URLs
- premium templates or subscription tier
